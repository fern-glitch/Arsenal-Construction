Who We Are

Meet
Armando

Arsenal Construction LLC was built on a simple idea: that great work speaks for itself. Founded and led by Armando — whose name literally means "one who builds" — Arsenal brings the same level of craft and commitment to every project, whether it's a kitchen remodel or a full renovation.

Based in the Woodburn and Salem area, we serve homeowners and businesses across the Willamette Valley. We show up when we say we will, communicate clearly, and deliver work you'll be proud of for years.

Our industry connections mean we can bring in the right trades at the right time — keeping your project moving without the runaround.
